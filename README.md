# Netflix Content Strategy & Business Analytics

> An end-to-end Data Analytics and Business Analytics project that explores Netflix's global content library using Python. This project demonstrates the complete data analysis workflow, from data cleaning and feature engineering to visualization and business recommendations.

---

#  Project Overview

Netflix is one of the world's largest streaming platforms with thousands of Movies and TV Shows from different countries and genres.

The objective of this project is to analyze Netflix's content library, uncover hidden trends, answer business questions, and provide actionable recommendations using Python.

This project demonstrates practical Data Analytics skills used by Data Analysts in real-world business environments.

---

#  Business Problem

Netflix continuously adds new content to its platform. Understanding content trends, genre popularity, regional distribution, and audience preferences is essential for making strategic business decisions.

This project aims to answer questions such as:

- Which content type dominates Netflix?
- Which countries contribute the most content?
- Which genres are most popular?
- How has Netflix's content library grown over time?
- Which ratings are most common?
- Which months have the highest content additions?
- What business strategies can Netflix adopt based on historical trends?

#  Key Business Insights

- Netflix's catalog is dominated by Movies over TV Shows.
- The United States contributes the highest number of titles.
- Drama and International Movies are among the most popular genres.
- TV-MA is the most common content rating.
- Netflix experienced rapid content growth after 2015.
- Content additions show seasonal release patterns.
- Most movies have moderate runtimes, indicating a focus on standard-length feature films.

---

#  Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis:

- Increase investment in high-performing genres such as Drama and International Movies.
- Expand original productions in emerging markets to improve regional diversity.
- Optimize release schedules using historical monthly trends.
- Strengthen collaborations with consistently successful directors.
- Maintain a balanced mix of Movies and TV Shows to meet audience preferences.
